Section 01

Project Overview

A regional real estate firm with a buyer and seller database of 4,200+ opted-in contacts needed a reliable way to push time-sensitive listing alerts, open house announcements, and market update campaigns via SMS — without the per-message fees and compliance limitations of generic bulk SMS platforms.

We built a custom campaign management system that gave the firm full control over contact segmentation, message scheduling, opt-out compliance, and delivery analytics — all hosted on their own infrastructure.

Section 02

The Challenge

The client was using a third-party SMS marketing platform that charged $0.04/message, provided no native CRM integration, and offered limited control over opt-out workflows. At their send volume, monthly platform costs were $800–$1,200, and any opt-out that slipped through the cracks represented a potential TCPA violation and significant legal exposure.

  • Per-message pricing made high-volume campaigns cost-prohibitive for routine market updates
  • No CRM sync — contact lists had to be exported, cleaned, and re-uploaded for every campaign
  • Opt-out handling was manual — a compliance liability requiring a dedicated staff review each week
  • No scheduling logic — all campaigns had to be manually triggered during business hours
Section 03

The Solution

We built a full-stack campaign management platform backed by Twilio Messaging Services, with an internal web dashboard for the firm's marketing team to create, segment, schedule, and monitor campaigns without technical assistance.

  • Contact Management: Segmented contact database by buyer/seller status, zip code, price range, and opt-in timestamp — enabling targeted sends rather than full-list blasts
  • Automated Opt-Out Handling: STOP keyword processing updates the contact record in real-time; re-opt-in flow via START is fully automated and logged with timestamp
  • Campaign Scheduler: Campaigns can be queued for specific send windows (e.g., weekdays 9am–6pm only) with rate-limiting to respect carrier throughput limits
  • Delivery Reporting Dashboard: Real-time delivery status, failed message alerts, unsubscribe rate, and response rate by campaign — all in a single view
  • Template Library: Pre-approved message templates with merge fields (first name, property address, price) reduce compose time and ensure brand consistency
Section 04

Technical Architecture

Section 05

Key Results

Platform operational costs reduced from $800–$1,200/month to approximately $180/month in Twilio usage — an 80% cost reduction at the same send volume.

  • Delivery rate of 98.4% across all campaigns — above industry average of 94–96%
  • Average campaign response rate of 31% — driven by segmentation replacing full-list blasting
  • Zero TCPA compliance incidents since launch — automated opt-out processing eliminated the manual review requirement
  • Marketing team campaign creation time dropped from 3 hours to 22 minutes using the template library and saved segments
  • Platform has processed over 280,000 messages since deployment with no carrier filtering events
